About Us

Evanatus Team – Shaping the Future of Wireless EV Charging

Imagine a world with no messy cables, no waiting in long lines at charging stations, and no wasted time. A world where you simply park your electric vehicle, and it charges automatically—wirelessly—while you go on with your day.

This is the vision that brought the Evanatus Team to life. We are not just a team; we are a group of dreamers and innovators determined to push the boundaries of clean energy and smart transportation. Our mission is to develop a fully automated wireless charging system for electric vehicles—a solution that could revolutionize the way the world thinks about mobility and sustainability.

If brought to life, this technology will save time, eliminate hassle, and most importantly, take us a step closer to a greener, cleaner, and smarter future.

And what makes it even more special? This journey begins right here at Damietta University, Faculty of Engineering, where a group of passionate students decided to transform an idea into reality—an idea that could leave a lasting impact on the entire world.

We may just be at the starting line, but we truly believe that every great innovation starts with a single bold step. And this is ours.
